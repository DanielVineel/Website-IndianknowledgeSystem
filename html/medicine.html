Types of Medicine in India

Type of Medicine Origin Key Concepts Primary Region
Ayurveda India, over 5,000 years ago Balance of body, mind, and spirit; three doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha); herbal remedies; lifestyle & diet All India
Yoga India, over 5,000 years ago Physical postures (Asanas), breath control (Pranayama), meditation, ethical principles All India
Unani Greece → Arab/Persian → India (medieval period) Four humors (blood, phlegm, yellow bile, black bile); holistic approach; herbal remedies; diet & lifestyle India (esp. North India, Mughal influence)
Siddha South India (Tamil Nadu), over 5,000 years ago Three humors (Vata, Pitta, Kapha); herbal & mineral medicines; lifestyle & spiritual health Tamil Nadu, South India
Naturopathy Europe (19th-20th century), developed in India) Natural healing, diet, exercise, hydrotherapy, massage, and lifestyle changes All India (wellness centers)
Homeopathy Germany, 18th century; introduced to India in 19th century “Like cures like”; highly diluted natural substances; holistic treatment All India
